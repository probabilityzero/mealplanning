Dawn — Noon — Dusk • Compact Nutrition Map

Dawn — Drink (minor)
  • water + pinch of salt or lemon
  • jaggery water
  • black coffee / tea (no sugar)
  • curd drink (lassi)
  • sattu water
Dawn — Meal (major)
  • 4 slices bread + 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • banana + milk or curd
  • poha with peanuts
  • oats with banana
  • roti + curd
Tip: choose one minor + one major. Minor stabilises electrolytes; major supplies bulk calories/fat.
Noon — Snack (minor)
  • cucumber or tomato slices + salt/lemon
  • buttermilk
  • small fruit (banana, guava, papaya)
  • roasted chana or handful peanuts
Noon — Meal (major)
  • 500 g cooked rice + soya or chicken (~150 g raw equiv.)
  • rice + dal + potato + leafy veg
  • khichdi with ghee
  • 2 chapati + sabzi + curd
  • rice + small mutton/fish + peas
Tip: if no kitchen, buy cooked rice+veg or cheap protein plate; add cucumber or fruit for micronutrients.
Dusk — Meal (major)
  • 500 g cooked rice + chicken (150–200 g raw equiv.)
  • paneer + rice + light oil drizzle
  • dal + rice + spinach
  • chapati + sabzi + mutton/fish
  • poha + curd + peanuts (lighter option)
Tip: keep this the largest meal (dusk) for recovery/repair. Add a cucumber or curd side for digestion.
